Fine art prints - Price $$$

Fine art paper is a professional quality one, made out natural fibers. The surface layer of this paper absorbes the inks well and the result is a rich, contrasty, saturated image. It's a bit more expensive but trust me, the results are worth it, specially on landscape themes or any image that requieres a wide tonality range.

Foamboard PVC - Price $$

PVC sheet is a versatile, foamed material made out of plastics. Foamboard sheets are waterproof and resistant to water and heat. Printing over this material guarantees durability and resistance to bad weather. You can forget about buying a frame, as the board is about 1,5 cm thick. The main drawback its the colors, a bit less saturated than fineart.
